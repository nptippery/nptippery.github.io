Limnanthemum minimum F.Muell.
- von Mueller, F. 1858. Fragmenta Phytographiae Australiae 1:40.
Foliis parvis cordatis subsessilibus subtus bullato-rugosis, lobis obtusis patulis, sinu baseos profundo, pedunculis e nodo fasciculatis filiformibus, calycis segmentis angusto-lanceolatis capsulam aequantibus, corollae parvae albae laciniis denticulatis obovo-oblongis, fundo albo fimbriato, stylo brevi, stigmate nudo, capsula 6-12-sperma ovata, seminibus minimis pallidis compresso-globosis carinatis tuberculatis. Leaves small, cordate, sub-sessile, blistered-rugose below, with open, obtuse lobes, basal sinus deep. Peduncles filiform, bundled from the node. Calyx segments narrow-lanceolate, equaling the capsule. Corolla small, white, with denticulate lobes, obovate-oblong, with a white, fimbriate throat. Style short, stigma bare. Capsule 6- to 12-seeded, ovate. Seeds very small, pale, compressed-globose, keeled, tuberculate.
In stagnis aqua limpida repletis flumen Fitzmaurice versus. In still, clear water toward the river Fitzmaurice.
Folia pollice saepe breviora subtus utplurimum rufa. Corolla 5- raro 4-partita, diametro quatuor linearum. Leaves 1 in [2.5 cm], often shorter, [utplurimum] red underneath. Corolla five-, rarely four-parted, 4 lin [8.5 mm] in diameter.

Villarsia geminata R.Br.
- Brown R. 1810. Prodromus Florae Novae Hollandiae. p. 457.
Capsula evalvis. Repens, foliis alte cordatis integris, racemis oppositifoliis: pedicellis geminatis, capsula calycem aequante, seminibus margine nudis. Capsule evalvate. Creeping, leaves deeply cordate, entire. Raceme with opposite leaves. Pedicels paired. Capsule of equal length as calyx. Seeds with a bare margin.
α. Folia subcoriacea subtus saepius discolora diametero 1-2-unciali. α. Leaves subcoriaceous, underneath discolored, diameter 1-2 unciali [2-5 cm].
β. Folia membranacea paginis concoloribus diametro 6-10-lineari. Forsan distincta, quandoque acaulis. β. Leaves membranaceous, both sides of the same color, diameter 6-10 lin [12-21 mm]. Perhaps distinct, and if so acauline.

Nymphoides krishnakesara K.T.Joseph & Sivar.
- Joseph, K.T., Sivarajan, V.V. 1990. A new species of Nymphoides (Menyanthaceae) from India. Nordic Journal of Botany 10(3):281.
Nymphoidis macrospermi Vasudevan similis in habitu dioecio, sed in statura minore, rhizomate verticali, foliis dimorphis, floribus masculanis filamentis erectis, antheribus azureis, pistillodio sine pilis stigmaticis, floribus femineis pistillo verticillis duobus pilis stigmaticis radiatis, fructu seminibus 5-10 lateraliter complanatis muricatis differt. Similar in dioecious habit to Nymphoides macrosperma Vasudevan, but differs in smaller stature, vertical rhizome, dimorphic leaves, masculine flowers with erect filaments, blue anthers, pistillode without stigmatic hairs, feminine flowers with a pistil with two vertical, radiate stigmatic hairs, fruit with 5-10 seeds flattened laterally and muricate.
Type: Joseph 43001, India. Kerala, Cannanore Dist. Madai. Oct. 1988 (MH holotype, Z. CALI isotypes).

N. Forbesiana (Griseb.) Kuntze affinis, sed minutior, annua, stolonifera, foliis ovatis, floribus seminibusque minoribus, glandibus interstaminalibus sessilibus, breviter villosis. Affinity with N. forbesiana (Griseb.) Kuntze, but smaller, annual, stoloniferous, with ovate leaves, smaller flowers and seeds, sessile interstaminal glands that are shortly villous.

Typus: Milne-Redhead 4317, Mwinilunga Distr., half a mile SW of Matonchi Farm, 24. 1. 1938 (K).

Nymphoides fallax Ornduff
- Ornduff, R. 1969. Neotropical Nymphoides (Menyanthaceae): Meso-American and West Indian species. Brittonia 21:348.
Herbae aquaticae perennes (interdum annuae?) rhizomatosae foliis natantibus; foliorum laminae late ovatae vel orbiculares, 4-10 (-12) cm diametro, superne virides et infra aliquando fuscatae; petioli foliorum vegetativorum 10-75 cm longi vel plurorum, petiolus folii inflorescentiae 0.5-2.5 cm longi et apice caulis petioloidi; inflorescentia 5-20 floribus, fasciculo bracteato congesto; alabastra capsulaeque submersae; flores pantameri ephemeri emersi pedicellis 2-5 (-7) cm longis; sepala 6-7 (-10) mm longa; petala oblonga lutea, 12-15 mm longa, pubescentia copiose, alis fimbriatis trichomatum connatorum marginalium; petala caespite stipitato trichomatum basi; stylus floris longistyli 4-5 mm longus stigmate 2-4-lobato carnoso apicali; stylus floris brevistyli brevior stigmate subsessili; stamina floris longistyli filamentis brevibus antherisque cremeis 1.4-1.7 mm longis; stamina floris brevistyli filamentis longioribus antherisque cremeis 1.9-2.5 mm longis; capsulae ovoidea-globosae 5-7 mm longae; 10-25 semina 2-2.5 mm diametro, tuberculata vel fere laevia, brunnea vel pallide brunnea, modice compressa; chromosomatum numerus n = 18; florentes in menses omnia. Aquatic perennial (sometimes annual?), rhizomatous herb with floating leaves. Laminae of leaves broadly ovate or orbicular, 4-10 (-12) cm in diameter, green above and sometimes darkened beneath. Petioles of vegetative leaves 10-75 cm long or more. Petiole of inflorescence leaf 0.5-2.5 cm long and petioloid at the apex of the stem. Inflorescence with 5-20 flowers, in a congested, bracteate fascicle. [Alabastra] and capsules submerged. Flowers pentamerous, ephemeral, emersed, with pedicels 2-5 (-7) cm long. Sepals 6-7 (-10) mm long. Petals oblong, yellow, 12-15 mm long, with copious pubescence, with fimbriate wings of connate marginal trichomes. Petals with stipitate hair at the base of the trichomes. Style of longistyle flower 4-5 mm long, with a two- to four-lobed, apical, fleshy stigma. Style of brevistyle flower shorter, with a subsessile stigma. Stamens of longistyle flower with short filaments and cream-colored anthers, 1.4-1.7 mm long. Stamens of brevistyle flower with longer filaments and cream-colored anthers, 1.9-2.5 mm long. Capsules ovoid-globose, 5-7 mm long. Seeds 10-25, 2.0-2.5 mm in diameter, tuberculate or perhaps smooth, brown or pale brown, moderately compressed. Chromosome number n = 18. Flowering in all months.
Typus: Mexico: Chiapas: reservoir of Rancho Nuevo on road to Huistán, Municipio de San Cristóbal de las Casas, field collected 26 Apr 1965, Breedlove & Raven s.n. (Holotype: [cultivated specimen] UC-M 200492; Isotype: DS.)

Villarsia minor D.Don ex G.Don
- Don, G. 1837. A General System of Gardening and Botany (General History of Dichlamydeous Plants). v. 4 p. 167-169.
Natant; leaves reniformly orbicular, smooth beneath; having the hind lobes divaricate; peduncles smooth, short; capsule globose, crustaceous. Native of the Himalaiah. Plant 4 times smaller in every part than V. Indica.

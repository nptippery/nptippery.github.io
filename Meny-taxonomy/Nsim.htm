Nymphoides simulans Aston
- Aston, H.I. 2002. Nymphoides simulans (Menyanthaceae): a new species from northern Australia. Muelleria 16:83-86.
Nymphoides spongiosa Aston simulans arcte habitu, foliis, fructis et seminibus, sed floribus 4-partitis (interdum 3-partitis vel 5-partitis) homostylis, antheris stigmata aequantibus praecipue differt. Closely similar to Nymphoides spongiosa Aston in habit, leaves, fruit and seeds, but differs principally in four-parted (sometimes three-parted or five-parted), homostylous flowers, anthers equaling the stigma.

N. parvifoliae (Griseb.) Kuntze simillima, recedit habitu copiosus ramoso, ramis saepe proliferantibus, rhizomate sarmentoso, foliis natantibus distincte nervosis, floribus pluribus per fasciculum, lobis corollae profuse fimbriatis appendicibus longis, capilliformibus, antheris altitudine sinuum corollae positis, et fructibus majoribus seminisque pluribus (15-20).
Closely similar to N. parvifolia (Griseb.) Kuntze, but differing in its more profusely branching habit with branches often proliferating, rhizome with runners, distinctly veined floating leaves, larger number of flowers in each cluster, corolla lobes profusely fimbriate with long hair-like fimbriae, anthers borne on level with the sinus of the corolla and larger fruits with more (15-20) seeds.

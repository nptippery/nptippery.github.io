Limnanthemum verrucosum R.E.Fries
- Fries, R.E. 1907. Zur Kenntnis der Phanerogamenflora der Grenzgebiete zwischen Bolivia un Argentinien. III. Einige gamopetale Familien. Arkiv för Botanik, Stockholm 6(11):1-31.
Nymphaeanthe foliis orbicularibus, basi profunde cordatis, altero laminae lobo basali alterum tegente, margine integerrimis, supra glaberrimis et subtus glandulis densis punctatis, carnosulis (ad 1 mm. crassis) et fragilibus, siccis membranaceis, 7-12 cm. diam.; nervis tribus, subtus vix conspicuis. Inflorescentia ad 10-flora, pedunculis circ. 4 cm. longis. Calycis lobis oblongis, obtusis, 6 mm. longis et 2 mm. latis. Corolla alba et basi pallide flava, calyce subduplo longiore, 13-14 mm. longa, laciniis margine et supra dense et longe albido-fimbriatis, atque basi coronula fimbriarum instructis. Stylo elongato, stigmate subcapitato, crispo. Capsula oblongo-ovoidea, apice stylo persistente apiculata, ca. 6 mm. longa et 3.5 mm. diam., calycem subaequante. Seminibus globoso-ovoideis, paulo complanatis, olivaceis, verrucosis, 1-1¼ mm. longis, ¾-1 mm. latis. [sect.?] Nymphaeanthe. Leaves orbicular, deeply cordate at the base, with one basal lobe of the lamina covering the other, entire at the margin, above very glabrous and below punctate with dense glands, rather fleshy (to 1 mm thick) and fragile, membranaceous when dry, 7-12 cm in diameter. Veins three, hardly conspicuous below. Inflorescence up to 10-flowered, with peduncles around 4 cm long. Calyx lobes oblong, obtuse, 6 mm long and 2 mm broad. Corolla white and pale yellow at the base, less than twice longer than the calyx, 13-14 mm long, with fringes at the margin and on the upper surface densely and longly white-fimbriate, and with a coronula of fringes at the base. Style elongate, stigma subcapitate, curled. Capsule oblong-ovoid, apiculate with a persistent style at the apex, around 6 mm long and 3.5 mm in diameter, sub-equaling the calyx. Seeds globose-ovoid, a little flattened, olive-colored, verrucose, 1-1.25 mm long, 0.75-1 mm broad.
Bolivia: San Luis (inter Tarija et Chaco), in aqua 3-4 dm. alta [6/3 02; Fr. 1334].
Die Art scheint mir dem auf Cuba vorkommenden Limnanthemum Grayanum Griseb. am nächsten zu stehen. Sie unterscheidet sich jedoch von der Beschreibung un dem Typusexemplare dieser Art durch die im Verhältnis zur Frucht kürzeren Kelchzipfel, durch die grösseren und mit niedrigeren, zerstreuten Höckern versehen Samen, durch die weisse Farbe der Blüte u.s.w. Von L. Humboldtianum (Kunth) Kuntze zeigt sie grössere Unterscheide, z.B. in der Blattform (mit einander deckenden Basallappen), in der Behaarung der Kronenblätter (die ganze Oberseite dicht gewimpert) und in der warzigen Oberfläche der Samen. The species seems to me to stand next to Limnanthemum grayanum Griseb., occurring on Cuba. It differs, however from the description of that type specimen of that species by the calyx division, by the larger and with lower, shorter peaks in relation to the fruit, absent-minded provides seeds, by the white color of the flower, etc. From L. humboldtianum (Kunth) Kuntze it shows greater difference, e.g. with respect to the leaf form (with basal lobes covering each other), in the hairiness of the corolla lobes (the whole top side densely covered) and with respect to the tubercular surface of the seeds.

Limnanthemum coreanum H.Lév.
- Léveille, H. in Fedde, F. 1910. Decades plantarum novarum. Repertorium specierum novarum regni vegetabilis 8:284-285.
Planta radicans vel stolonifera; folia radicalia ex aquae altitudine petiolata, numerosa orbicularia profunde et anguste cordata integra glabra, 3-4 x 3-4 cm; flores in umbellas densas et sessiles secus caulem et stolones distributi; pedicelli floribus longiores; sepala lanceolata obtusa quam capsula matura dimidio breviora; petala flavida? sepalis longiora; semina globosa levia flava nitida. Rooting or stoloniferous plant. Radical leaves petiolate according to the depth of water, numerous, orbicular, deeply and narrowly cordate, entire, glabrous, 3-4 x 3-4 cm. Flowers in dense and sessile umbels distributed [secus] the stem and stolons. Pedicels longer than the flowers. Sepals lanceolate, obtuse, shorter by half than the mature capsule. Petals yellow? longer than the sepals. Seeds globose, smooth, yellow, shining.
Corea; Quelpaert in oryzetis vel aquis Hannon, 27 août - 30 oct. 1908 (Taquet 1516, 1518). Korea: Quelpaert in rice fields or waters of Hannon, 27 Aug - 30 Oct 1908 (Taquet 1516, 1518).

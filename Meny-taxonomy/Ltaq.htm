Limnanthemum taquetii H.Lév.
- Léveille, H. in Fedde, F. 1910. Decades plantarum novarum. Repertorium specierum novarum regni vegetabilis 8:284-285.
Forma lacuum et stagnorum radice robustissima, petiolis multo magis elongatis, limbo 7-8 cm tam longo quam lato; petiolis planis 4-6 mm latis; sepala acuta capsulam maturam aequantia; semina ut in praecedenti [L. coreanum]. Form of lakes and swamps with a very robust root. Petioles much more elongate. Blade 7-8 cm, as long as broad. Petioles flat, 4-6 mm broad. Sepals acute, equaling the mature capsule. Seeds as in the preceding species [L. coreanum].
Corea; Quelpaert, in stagno Lineum, 14 sept. 1908 (Taquet, 1519). Korea: Quelpaert, in Lineum swamp, 14 Sep 1908 (Taquet 1519).

Nymphoides subacuta Aston
- Aston, H. 1982. New Australian species of Nymphoides Séguier (Menyanthaceae). Muelleria 5:35-51.
Plantae annuae vel ?perennes. Laminae foliorum angustissime ad late ovatae, aliquando quasicirculares, integrae, profunde cordatae, acutae vel late obtusae, (1-)3-11 x (0.5-)2-9 cm. Petiolus compressus, in sectione oblongus. Inflorescentia laxa, binis floribus pedicellatis binisque bracteis ad nodos; internodis 0.2-5(-9) cm longis. Flores heterostyli, (4)5(6)-partiti. Calyx projecturis labiformibus, incrassis minutis ad lobarum juncturas praeditus. Corolla (20-)26-40(-45) mm diametro, fluvo-aurantiaca; lobae alis lateralibus latis perlaciniatis fimbriaque transversa prope lobae basin papillarum tenuium liberarum formata praeditae. Papillae liberae, vel in fasuculis ad basin incrassatis semi-connatae, in medium marginemque fimbriam. Capsula ellipsoidea-ovoidea, 3-6 x 2.5-4 mm. Semina 2-8 per capsulam, ± globosa, leviter autem compressa, 1.4-1.9 x 1.3-1.7 x 1-1.4 mm (longitudo latitudinem aequans, crassitie 1⅓-1½ longior), nigrescentia-atrofusca maturitate, tholiformibus-projecturis velata; tholi de tuberculis tenuibus obtusis densi-appressis formati; depressiones inter tholis tuberculis similaribus brevioribus vestitae; caruncula basalis, circularis, pallida, crassa, conspicua. Annual or ?perennial plant. Leaf blades very narrow to broadly ovate, sometimes almost circular, entire, deeply cordate, acute or broadly obtuse, (1-)3-11 x (0.5-)2-9 cm. Petiole compressed, oblong in section. Inflorescence lax, with two pedicellate flowers and two bracts at the nodes; internodes 0.2-5(-9) cm long. Flowers heterostylous, (4)5(6)-parted. Calyx with labiform projections, furnished with minute thickenings at the junction of lobes. Corolla (20-)26-40(-45) mm in diameter, yellow-orange; lobes with broad, very laciniate wings and furnished with transverse fringe near the base of the lobe with the form of thin, free papillae. Papillae free, or in fascicles thickened at the base, semi-connate, into the middle and margine fringe. Capsule ellipsoid-ovoid, 3-6 x 2.5-4 mm. Seeds 2-8 per capsule, ± globose, but moderately compressed, 1.4-1.9 x 1.3-1.7 x 1-1.4 mm (length equalling breadth, 1.3-1.5 times longer than thickness), blackening to dark cloudy at maturity, covered in tholiform projections; tholi formed from thin, obtuse, densely-appressed tubercles; depressions between tholi covered with similar, shorter tubercles; caruncle basal, circular, pale, thick, conspicuous.

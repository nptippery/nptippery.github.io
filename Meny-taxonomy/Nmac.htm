Nymphoides macrosperma Vasudevan
- Vasudevan, R. 1968. A new species of Nymphoides (Menyanthaceae) from south India. Kew Bulletin 22: 101.
A ceteris speciebus indicis floribus dioecis, seminibus (tantum 2-6) duplo majoribus magis asymmetricis magis muriculatia, hilo majore obliquo differt. Differs from other Indian species in dioecious flowers, seeds (only 2-6) twice as large, more asymmetrical, more muricate, with a larger, oblique hilum.
Planta dioica aquatica, rhizomate perennante, ramis natantibus. Folia simplicia, orbicularia, cordata, natantia. Flores in ramis natantibus aggregati albi, unisexuales, bracteati, pedicellati, actinomorphi, hypogyni. Flores masculi: Calyx gamosepalus, in lacinias 5 spiraliter curvatas raro imbricatas 1.5 x 3 mm. alte divisus; corolla gamopetala, rotata, alba, in lacinias 5 oblongas acutas induplicatao-valvatas 2 x 6 mm. alte divisa, marginibus fimbriatis, superficie superiore vix pilosa; stamina 5, epipetala; filamentis albis, filiformibus, 2 mm. longis; antheris 1 mm. longis, 4-lobatis bilocularibus introrsis; polline luteolo, subsphaerico laevi parietibus tenuibus; pistillodio eminente simili pistillo fertili sed angustiore, ovulis abortivis, pilis stigmaticis brevibus. Flores feminei: calyx et corolla ut in flore masculo; staminodiis 5 forma et positione stamini similibus sed minutissimis albis; gynoecium ca. 4 mm., superius, bicarpellare, uniloculare, glandulis 5 basi aurantiacis ornatum; stylo brevi; stigmate bifido; lobis distincte fimbriatis; ovulis in utraque placenta 1-3 lenticularibus anatropis. Fructus ca. 6 x 8 mm., subglobosus, fusco-brunneus, muriculatus, endospermio carnoso, embryone recto paulum compresso. Dioecious aquatic plant, with perennating rhizome, floating branches. Leaves simple, orbicular, cordate, floating. Flowers aggregated on floating branches, white, unisexual, bracteate, pedicellate, actinomorphic, hypogynous. Masculine flowers: Calyx gamosepalous, divided into five spirally-curved, rarely imbricate lobes, 1.5 x 3 mm deep. Corolla gamopetalous, rotate, white, divided into five oblong, acute, induplicate-valvate lobes, 2 x 6 mm deep, with fimbriate margins, upper surface scarcely pilose. Stamens five, epipetalous; filaments white, filiform, 2 mm long; anthers 1 mm long, four-lobed, bilocular, introrse; pollen yellow, sub-spherical, smooth, with thin walls. Pistillode emanating like a fertile pistil but more narrow, with abortive ovules, short stigmatic hairs. Feminine flowers: Calyx and corolla as in masculine flowers. Staminodes five, in form and position similar to stamens, but very small, white. Gynoecium around 4 mm, superior, two-carpellate, unilocular, adorned with five orange glandules at the base; style short; stigma bifid; lobes distinctly fimbriate; ovules in each placenta 1-3, lenticular, anatropous. Fruit around 6 x 8 mm, sub-globose, dark-brown, finely muricate, with fleshy endosperm, with a straight, somewhat compressed embryo.

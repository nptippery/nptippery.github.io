Nymphoides tenuissima A.Raynal
- Raynal, A. 1971. Repartition geographique des Nymphoides (Menyanthaceae) Africains et Malgaches. Mitteilungen der botanischen Staatssammlung und des Instituts für systematische Botanik der Universität München 10: 124-134.
Herba annua stolonifera, habitu N. Milnei A. Raynal simulans, floribus albis parvis villosis homeostylis, glandibus interstaminalibus subnullis, glandibus hypogyniis nullis, stigmatibus bilobatis membranaceis laciniatis, capsula ovoidea longiore quam calyce, seminibus 5-11 ovatis complanatis, margine truncata plana. Annual, stoloniferous herb, similar in habit to N. milnei A. Raynal, with small, white, villous, homostylous flowers, subnull interstaminal glands, no hypogynous glands, two-lobed, membranaceous, laciniate stigmas, an ovoid capsule longer than the calyx, 5-11 ovate, compressed seeds, with a flat, truncate margin.
Typus: Mrs. H. M. Richards 9175, Zambie, Mporokoso Distr., Mweru-Wantipa Road to Bulayo, 1050 m, 12. 4. 1957. In shallow pond on mud among iron stone flat rocks. Flower white, petals fringed.

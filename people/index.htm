Nicholas P. Tippery, Ph.D.

  Dr. Tippery is a native of Denver, Colorado and has also lived in Texas, Washington, and Connecticut. He earned a dual Bachelor's degree in Biology and Classics from the University of Dallas in Irving, Texas, and a Ph.D. in Ecology and Evolutionary Biology from the University of Connecticut. When he is not chasing down elusive plants, Dr. Tippery enjoys hiking, bicycling, and volleyball, and he looks forward to embracing the Wisconsin winter!

About Dr. Tippery

Originally from Denver, Colorado, Nic has lived in Dallas, Seattle, and Connecticut. He joined the faculty of UW-Whitewater in 2011. He is broadly interested in plant function and evolution, and his research projects are split between field ecology and DNA-based laboratory work.
